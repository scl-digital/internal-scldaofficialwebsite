// 5-Year Strategic Roadmap

Where we're
headed.

A three-phase growth plan from market validation through ecosystem expansion — methodically scaling from a founder-led studio to a diversified digital enterprise.

Phase 01 · 2025–2026

Foundation & Market Validation

Establish the agency brand and operations stack, secure the first 5–15 paying clients, build 3–8 high-impact case studies, and achieve early financial stability. Build repeatable client acquisition pipelines and document core delivery systems into SOPs that outlast any individual.

Phase 02 · 2026–2028

Scale & Structural Systematization

Transition from founder-led to a fully structured operation. Grow to 30–100+ active accounts, shift revenue from project-based to Monthly Recurring Revenue retainers, build a dedicated core team, and penetrate high-growth verticals including Fintech and funded startups. Establish B2B alliances for white-label and referral pipelines.

Phase 03 · 2028–2030

Ecosystem Expansion & Productization

Evolve from a service agency to an asset-backed digital solutions enterprise. Launch proprietary SaaS products, secure multi-year institutional contracts, establish regional and international market presence, and explore strategic acquisitions of micro-agencies and proprietary tooling. Become a recognized thought leader in the African tech ecosystem.
