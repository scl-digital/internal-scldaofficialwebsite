// Step 01 — Discovery & Scoping

We start by understanding the real problem.

Before a single wireframe or line of code, we run a structured discovery process. We map your business model, user flows, existing tech, and competitive landscape. This surfaces the actual problem — not just the stated one. Most project failures trace back to misaligned scope. We fix that first.

// Deliverables
  • Problem statement & success metrics
  • Technical feasibility assessment
  • Project scope document & timeline
  • Risk identification & mitigation plan

// Step 02 — Strategy & Architecture

Architecture decisions made early save months later.

We design your technical and product strategy before touching a tool. Stack selection, system architecture, data models, integration map. The decisions made here determine how easily you can scale, pivot, and ship in the future. We treat architecture as a strategic asset, not just engineering scaffolding.

// Deliverables
  • Technology stack recommendation
  • System architecture diagram
  • Data model & integration plan
  • Go-to-market alignment document

// Step 03 — Design & Prototyping

We design in systems, not screens.

UI/UX design that prioritises clarity, conversion, and retention. We build interactive prototypes for validation before engineering begins — saving significant rework cost. Design is a thinking tool, not a decoration layer. We use it to stress-test assumptions and validate flows with real users before they're built.

// Deliverables
  • Full UI/UX design system
  • Interactive Figma prototype
  • User flow maps & journey analysis
  • Design QA & handoff documentation

// Step 04 — Build & Integrate

Production-grade engineering, shipped in iterations.

We build in 2-week sprint cycles with continuous deployment to staging. You see real progress — not PowerPoint updates. Every sprint ends with working, reviewable software. We maintain full test coverage, write documentation as we go, and keep your team in the loop at every stage.

// Deliverables
  • Sprint-based delivery with demo sessions
  • Staging environment & QA pipeline
  • Code review & documentation
  • Integration testing & sign-off

// Step 05 — Launch & Optimise

Launching is the start, not the finish.

We manage production deployment, post-launch monitoring, and the critical first 30 days. Bugs get fixed fast, metrics get tracked from day one, and growth experiments begin immediately. For retained clients, we operate as an ongoing engineering and growth partner — shipping continuously rather than in large, risky releases.

// Deliverables
  • Production deployment & monitoring setup
  • Analytics & dashboard configuration
  • 30-day post-launch support
  • Growth experiment roadmap

Engagement models

⚡

Sprint Project

Fixed-scope delivery across 4–12 weeks. Ideal for MVPs, new features, or defined digital products with a clear outcome in mind.

Best for: Startups, new product launches, defined scope work
Timeline: 4 – 12 weeks
Pricing: Fixed project fee
🔁

Retained Partner

An embedded team operating on monthly retainer. You get continuous delivery, strategic input, and a team that knows your product inside out.

Best for: Scaling companies, ongoing product evolution
Timeline: 3+ month minimum
Pricing: Monthly retainer
🏗

Team Augmentation

Senior engineers, designers, and strategists embedded into your existing team. Fill capability gaps without the overhead of permanent hiring.

Best for: Teams with gaps, scale-ups hiring slowly
Timeline: Flexible
Pricing: Daily / monthly rate
