// WHY JOIN US

More than a
job.

At SCL Digital Agency, you're not filling a seat — you're co-building something real. We give our people ownership, creative latitude, and direct exposure to how a modern digital business is built from the ground up.

Ownership from Day One
You won't be waiting six months before you touch real work. From your first week you'll be shipping, designing, or advising on live projects that matter to real clients.
Accelerated Learning
Our lean structure means broad exposure. You'll work across engineering, design, strategy, and AI — building skills that would take years to develop in a siloed corporate setting.
Built for Growth
We are mid-scale by design. Phase 02 of our 5-year roadmap targets 30–100 active clients. The people who join now grow with the business — into leadership roles, equity conversations, and beyond.
Remote-Friendly, Africa-Rooted
Headquartered in Cape Town with a hub in Kigali, we operate across time zones and borders. Collaboration is async-first, output-focused, and internationally minded.
// CULTURE & IMPACT

How we
actually work.

A
No-bloat philosophy
Every line of code, every design decision, every strategy recommendation must earn its place. We obsess over simplicity because our clients' businesses depend on it.
B
Direct feedback, always
We don't do performative positivity. Feedback is clear, specific, and given with respect. We critique the work, never the person — and we mean it.
C
Ship, then improve
Production-grade from day one doesn't mean perfect from day one. We ship, we learn, we iterate. Speed and quality are not enemies — poor planning is.
D
Africa-forward ambition
We're building a category-defining digital agency from the African continent. That context shapes how we think about markets, talent, and the problems worth solving.

// HOW WE HIRE

Simple.
Fast. Fair.

STEP 01
Apply
Submit your CV, portfolio, or a note about why you want in. No cover letter essays required — just show us who you are and what you've built.
STEP 02
Quick Screen
A 20-minute async or live call with the team lead. We check for alignment on values and ways of working — not just technical keywords.
STEP 03
Practical Task
A short, real-world task relevant to the role. We time-box it deliberately — it should not take more than 2–3 hours. We pay for lengthy assessments.
STEP 04
Decision
We move fast. Within 5 business days of your final interview, you'll have a clear answer — and honest feedback either way.
